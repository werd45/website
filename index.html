University of June Lenard Continues to Strengthen Prevention and Control of Covid-19

The University of June Lenard’ Crisis Management Team, one with the government in fighting the spread of the COVID-19 disease, continues to reinforce measures and strategies to minimize possible virus transmission in the university. A re-orientation webinar on COVID-19 protocols was conducted last March 30, 2021, 10:00AM via private Facebook group. All teaching and non-teaching personnel were enjoined to watch the webinar to be reminded again of the do’s and don’ts during these challenging times.
